Drivetrain Models

Powertrain Classifications

An analytical comparison of the four prominent electric vehicle systems.

BEV

Battery Electric

100% Electric drivetrain driven solely by battery grids charged from external plugs.

  • Zero direct emissions
  • Maximum running savings
  • Range: 150 - 450+ miles
PHEV

Plug-in Hybrid

Integrates an electric motor battery with a gasoline engine backup. Plugs in to charge.

  • Gas engine long backup
  • 25-55 mi electric range
  • Flexible hybrid power
HEV

Hybrid Electric

Uses gas engine alongside motor help. Recharges battery solely via braking friction.

  • No charging plug needed
  • Higher MPG than pure gas
  • Short electric capabilities
FCEV

Fuel Cell Electric

Feeds hydrogen gas into a fuel cell stack to generate electricity for motor drives.

  • Refuels under 5 minutes
  • Emits only clean water
  • Sparse refueling stations

Engine Flow

How Drivetrains Work

Electric motors eliminate complex transmissions and spark chambers, running on three modular concepts.

1. The Grid Battery

Stores power as Direct Current (DC). Integrated flat beneath the passenger cabin floor, this design creates a low center of gravity to optimize rollover safety and cornering.

2. Inverter Conversion

Converts direct current (DC) into alternating current (AC) for motors. Controls speed and torque by managing frequency ranges.

3. Electric Drive Motor

Converts magnetic fields into mechanical rotation directly at the wheels. Delivers instantaneous torque from a complete stop with 90%+ energy efficiency rates.

4. Regenerative Recovery

Slashes brake wear. Reversing the magnetic poles turns the motor into a generator when slowing down, recovering kinetic energy back into battery storage.

Charging Speeds Comparison

A clear breakdown of the three primary charging levels available to electric drivers.

Level 1 Charging

120V AC Outlet

Standard household power plug. Slow speeds, suited for plug-in hybrids or low daily commuters.

Speed: 3-5 miles range / hour

Ideal for: Home Overnight

Level 2 Charging

240V AC Charger

High-capacity wall units installed in residential garages, retail parking structures, or offices.

Speed: 20-30 miles range / hour

Ideal for: Home / Work chargers

DC Fast Charging

480V+ Direct Current

High-voltage chargers directly refilling batteries. Primarily located along highway routes for travel.

Speed: 100-250+ miles in 20 min

Ideal for: Road Trips
