Financial Incentives

Clean Vehicle Tax Credits

Legislations like the Inflation Reduction Act allow new EV buyers to apply up to **$7,500** in tax credits directly at point-of-sale dealership checkouts. Used EV credits provide up to **$4,000** for pre-owned electric cars.

Dealership Discount

Apply tax credits directly as a down-payment rebate when buying, rather than waiting for annual tax filings.

Used Vehicle Rebates

Receive 30% off (capped at $4,000) for pre-owned electric models priced under $25,000.

Wall Charger Credits

Get up to a 30% federal credit (up to $1,000) on installation labor and Level 2 smart hardware.

Credit Compliance Guidelines

To qualify for federal tax credits, vehicles and buyers must satisfy these conditions:

  • MSRP Capping Electric SUVs, Trucks, and Vans must be under $80,000. Sedans and Hatchbacks must be under $55,000.
  • Adjusted Gross Income Limit Single filers must earn under $150,000; Married Joint filers must earn under $300,000.
  • Battery Sourcing Check Min. percentage of battery core minerals must be processed or sourced in the US or partner countries.
  • Assembly Origin Final mechanical assembly must take place within North American borders.
